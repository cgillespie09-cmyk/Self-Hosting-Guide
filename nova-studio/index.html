Questions

Good to know before we start.

What's included in the $50/month retainer?

Hosting management, security and software updates, monthly backups, uptime monitoring, and small content edits (up to 30 minutes of changes per month). It's optional but keeps your site healthy and you off the hook for the technical side.

How long does it take to build my site?

Standard sites typically launch in 2–3 weeks. Advanced sites with more pages or custom functionality take about 10–14 business days from final content approval.

Do I own my website?

Yes — completely. The site, the domain, and the content are yours. The monthly retainer is optional maintenance, not a license to keep your own site running.

What if I need more pages than my package includes?

No problem — additional pages can be added to either package for a flat per-page rate, quoted before any work begins.

Can I start with Standard and upgrade later?

Absolutely. A lot of clients start with Standard and add pages, animations, or booking/e-commerce functionality once the business grows.
