Our Story

Cooking with the seasons, not the trends.

Lumière began as a single table in the back of a wine shop. Today it's a 32-seat dining room built around one idea: cook what's good right now, source it as close to home as possible, and never let a dish leave the kitchen we wouldn't serve our own family.

Chef Antoine Marchand leads a small kitchen team that changes the menu with the seasons — never the standard.
